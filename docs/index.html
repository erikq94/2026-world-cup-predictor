I Built a World Cup Predictor

I trained an XGBoost model on 30,000+ international matches plus player stats, then ran the entire 2026 World Cup through 50,000 Monte Carlo simulations to see who lifts the trophy. Scroll through — from the group stage all the way to my predicted champion.
